Peanut-Butter Thost....

A perfect blend of taste and energy, this toast pairs creamy peanut butter with sweet banana slices for a rich, wholesome bite. Born from simple kitchen creativity, it’s now a go-to snack for both health lovers and comfort seekers alike...


Start your day with Peanut Butter Banana Toast!..


Ingredients....

For Peanut Butter Toast:

  • Bread Slices – 4
  • Bananas – 2
  • Peanut Butter
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Maple Syrup
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Toast the bread slices until golden brown. Optionally, butter the bread before toasting for a richer flavor.
  2. Step 2: Spread a generous layer of peanut butter on each toasted slice.
  3. Step 3: Slice the bananas and arrange them evenly on top of the peanut butter.
  4. Step 4: Drizzle with maple syrup to add sweetness.
  5. Step 5: Sprinkle powdered sugar over the top for an extra touch.
  6. Step 6: Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Peanut Butter Banana Toast!
