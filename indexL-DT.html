Dal Tadka....

A true Indian comfort dish, Dal Tadka combines slow-cooked lentils with a sizzling tempering of ghee, garlic, and spices. The crackle of the tadka brings warmth, aroma, and a burst of flavor that defines homestyle cooking...


Start your day with Dal Tadka!..


Ingredients....

Dal Tadka:

  • Toor dal – 1/2 cup
  • Garlic – 1 tsp (for dal) + 1 tbsp (for tadka)
  • Ginger – 1 tsp
  • Coriander leaves – 1/4 cup + to garnish
  • Curry leaves – 12
  • Onion – 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Cumin seeds – 1/2 tsp
  • Bay leaves – 2
  • Green chilli – 2
  • Turmeric powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Red chilli powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1/2 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Ghee – 2 tbsp
  • Cooking oil – 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Wash and soak toor dal for 15 minutes. Pressure cook with water until soft.
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a pressure cooker, add cumin seeds, bay leaves, green chilli, curry leaves, and a pinch of hing (asafoetida). Sauté.
  3. Step 3: Add chopped garlic, ginger, onion, and fry until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Step 4: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Step 5: Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Step 6: Add the cooked dal and required water. Mix and cook for 6 whistles on medium heat.
  7. Step 7: Open the cooker and whisk dal to your preferred consistency. Adjust water if too thick.
  8. Step 8: Add fresh coriander leaves. Prepare tadka by heating ghee, frying chopped garlic and ginger until golden, and pouring it over the dal.
  9. Step 9: Optionally, use charcoal dhungar method for a restaurant aroma. Garnish with more coriander and serve hot.
