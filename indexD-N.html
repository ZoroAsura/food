Veg Hakka Noodles....

Stir-fried to perfection with crunchy vegetables and aromatic sauces, veg hakka noodles bring the bold flavors of Indo-Chinese street food to your plate. Every bite is a mix of spice, texture and irresistible taste...


Start your day with Veg Hakka Noodles!..


Ingredients....

Veg Hakka Noodles Ingredients:

  • Ching’s Just Soak Veg Hakka Noodles – 1 packet
  • Water – 5-6 cups
  • Capsicum – 1/4 cup
  • Onion – 1/4 cup
  • Cabbage – 1/4 cup
  • Spring onion – for garnish
  • Oil – 1 tbsp
  • Ching’s Chowmein Hakka Noodles Masala – 1 packet

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Boil 5-6 cups of water. Turn off the heat and soak the noodles. Cover and let stand 5 minutes.
  2. Step 2: While noodles soak, chop capsicum, onion, cabbage, and spring onion.
  3. Step 3: Drain water and rinse soaked noodles thoroughly.
  4. Step 4: Heat oil in a pan, sauté capsicum, onion, and cabbage for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Add noodles and Chowmein Hakka Noodles Masala. Mix well.
  6. Step 6: Add a small amount of water, stir and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Garnish with spring onion. Serve hot!
