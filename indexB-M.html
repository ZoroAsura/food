Milkshake....

BBlending milk, ice cream, and flavor into one glass, the milkshake was born in the early 1900s and quickly became a symbol of sweetness and nostalgia. Every sip feels like a cool reminder of simpler, happier times..


Start your day with Milkshake!..


Ingredients....

For Milkshake:

  • Cold milk – 2 cups
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate syrup – 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar – 2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Whipped cream or grated chocolate for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Pour 2 cups of cold milk into a blender.
  2. Step 2: Add 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder or chocolate syrup.
  3. Step 3: Add 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste).
  4. Step 4: Add ice cubes if you want a chilled shake.
  5. Step 5: Blend everything until smooth and frothy.
  6. Step 6: Pour into glasses and garnish with whipped cream or grated chocolate if desired.
  7. Step 7: Serve immediately and enjoy!
