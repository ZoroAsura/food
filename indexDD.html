Panner Tika

Panner Tika: Tender cubes of paneer, marinated in a rich blend of spices and yogurt, grilled to perfection, and simmered in a creamy, tangy tomato-based gravy. Each bite bursts with smoky, spicy, and savory flavors, making it a comforting, indulgent treat that’s perfect with naan or rice.

Chicken Tikka

Chicken Tikka: Juicy, marinated chicken pieces, char-grilled to perfection, then simmered in a creamy, spiced tomato gravy. Each bite is rich, smoky, and bursting with bold flavors—a comforting and indulgent dish best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Butter Chicken

Succulent chicken pieces cooked in a rich, creamy tomato and butter gravy, infused with mild spices and a hint of sweetness. Each bite is smooth, buttery, and full of flavor—a classic comfort dish perfect with naan or rice.Golden, crispy toast smothered in creamy peanut butter, topped with sweet, ripe banana slices. Each bite is a perfect harmony of nutty, sweet, and soft textures quick, delicious treat that feels like comfort on a plate.

Haka Noodles

Haka Noodles: Thin, springy noodles tossed with crisp vegetables, savory sauces, and aromatic spices. Each bite is flavorful, slightly tangy, and perfectly chewy—a quick, satisfying stir-fry that bursts with color and taste.
