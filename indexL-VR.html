Veg Fried Rice....

Born from Asian kitchens, veg fried rice turns simple ingredients into a flavorful masterpiece. Loaded with fresh vegetables and tossed in aromatic sauces, it’s the perfect blend of color, crunch, and comfort in every spoonful...


Start your day with Veg Fried Rice!..


Ingredients....

Veg Fried Rice:

  • Rice – 500 gm
  • Spring onion bulbs – 1/4 cup (chopped)
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Spring onion greens – 1/4 cup (chopped)
  • Green chilli paste – 1 tbsp
  • Carrots – 1/3 cup (chopped)
  • Cabbage – 1 cup (chopped)
  • Capsicum – 1/3 cup (chopped)
  • French beans – 1/4 cup (chopped)
  • Sugar – 1 tsp
  • Paneer – 150 gm (small dices)
  • Soy sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Red chilli sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Salt & pepper – to taste
  • Vinegar – 1 tsp
  • Spring onion greens – for garnish (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Wash rice to remove excess starch and soak it for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Boil water, add salt, oil, and soaked rice. Cook until 80-90% done (about 8-10 minutes). Drain extra water and keep aside.
  3. Step 3: Heat oil in a pan on high flame, add spring onion bulbs and ginger garlic paste. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Step 4: Add spring onion greens, green chilli paste, chopped veggies, and sugar. Sauté for 2-3 minutes on high flame.
  5. Step 5: Add paneer cubes and mix gently. Sauté for a minute.
  6. Step 6: Add soy sauce, red chilli sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix and cook for a minute on high heat.
  7. Step 7: Add cooked rice, mix gently, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Step 8: Add vinegar and chopped spring onion greens. Mix and toss well.
  9. Step 9: Serve hot, garnished with spring onion greens, and enjoy with schezwan sauce on the side.
