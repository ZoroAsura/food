Grilled Paneer Sandwich....

A fusion of Indian flavor and classic comfort, the grilled paneer sandwich layers spiced cottage cheese between crisp, buttery bread. Toasted to perfection, it’s a delicious mix of crunch, melt, and rich aroma in every bite...


Start your day with Grilled Paneer Sandwich!..


Ingredients....

Grilled Paneer Sandwich:

  • Paneer/Cottage cheese – 1 cup, grated
  • Carrot – 2 tbsp, grated
  • Capsicum – 2 tbsp, chopped
  • Corn – 2 tbsp, boiled
  • Coriander – 1 tbsp, finely chopped
  • Kashmiri red chilli powder – ¼ tsp
  • Cumin powder – ¼ tsp
  • Salt – ¼ tsp
  • Tomato sauce – 2 tbsp
  • Bread slices – 4 (white or brown)
  • Green chutney – 2 tsp
  • Butter – 2 tsp

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, take 1 cup grated paneer.
  2. Step 2: Add 2 tbsp carrot, 2 tbsp capsicum, 2 tbsp boiled corn, and 1 tbsp coriander.
  3. Step 3: Add ¼ tsp red chilli powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ¼ tsp salt, and 2 tbsp tomato sauce.
  4. Step 4: Mix well, making sure all the spices and veggies are evenly combined.
  5. Step 5: Spread green chutney on a bread slice.
  6. Step 6: Spread 2 tbsp of prepared paneer stuffing over the chutney.
  7. Step 7: Cover with another bread slice, also spread with green chutney.
  8. Step 8: Grill the sandwich or toast it on a tawa with butter until golden and crispy.
  9. Step 9: Cut in half and serve hot.
