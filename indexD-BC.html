Butter Chicken....

Marinated in spiced yogurt and grilled to perfection, paneer tikka is smoky, tender and bursting with flavor. A favorite from the tandoor, it captures the true spirit of Indian barbecue...


Start your day with Butter Chicken!..


Ingredients....

Butter Chicken Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast boneless – 300g
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Red chilli powder – 1 tbsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – to pan fry
  • Tomatoes, roughly cut – 500g
  • Onions, roughly cut – 100g
  • Garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Cashew nuts – 50g
  • Kasoori methi – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar – 4 tbsp
  • Kashmiri chilli powder – 2 tbsp
  • Butter – 5 tbsp
  • Cream – 3 tbsp
  • Malt vinegar / White Vinegar – 2 tbsp / 1.5 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Marinate chicken with ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, salt. Let rest for 15-20 mins.
  2. Step 2: Pan fry chicken pieces in heated oil until cooked. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: In the same pan, add onions, butter, then tomatoes and cashews. Add water, garlic paste, salt, malt vinegar, sugar, garam masala, and chilli powder. Mix well and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Blend this cooked mixture into a fine puree. Strain it back into the pan.
  5. Step 5: Add butter, cream, cooked chicken pieces, and kasoori methi. Simmer 5-7 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Garnish with a swirl of cream and kasoori methi. Serve hot!
