Chicken Tikka....

Juicy chicken pieces marinated in spiced yogurt and roasted over fire bring out a smoky, irresistible flavor. This timeless tandoori favorite is a celebration of spice, heat and perfection in every bite...


Start your day with Chicken Tikka!..


Ingredients....

Chicken Tikka Ingredients:

  • Boneless Chicken – 500 gms (2” tikka pieces)
  • Hung Curd/Yoghurt – 5 tbsp
  • Turmeric Powder – pinch (1/8 tsp)
  • Kashmiri Chilli Powder – 5 tsp
  • Garam Masala Powder – 3/4 tsp
  • Kasuri Methi (dry roasted & powdered) – 1/2 tsp
  • Salt – 1 tsp
  • Lemon Juice – 1.5 tsp
  • Ginger Garlic Paste – 1 tbsp
  • Cooking Oil – 2.5 tbsp + for pan frying

    • Gravy Ingredients:

  • Onions, fine chopped – 2 medium
  • Ginger Garlic Paste – 2 tsp
  • Tomato purée – 1 cup
  • Fresh Cream – 6 tbsp
  • Turmeric Powder – 1/4 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder – 1 tsp
  • Coriander Powder – 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Garam Masala Powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Kasuri Methi (roasted & crushed) – 1 tsp
  • Refined Oil – 3 tbsp
  • Salt – for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Marinate chicken with curd, turmeric, chilli, garam masala, kasuri methi, salt, lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, and oil. Mix well, cover and refrigerate 1-2 hours (30 minutes outside before cooking).
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a pan and pan-fry the chicken tikkas in batches till lightly browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: For the gravy, heat oil in a pan, fry onions till lightly brown. Add ginger garlic paste, sauté, then add tomato purée.
  4. Step 4: Add turmeric, red chilli, coriander, cumin powders, and salt. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Add fried tikkas, 300 ml water, garam masala, mix, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Add fresh cream and roasted kasuri methi, mix and cook on low for 2 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice!
