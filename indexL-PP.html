Palak Paneer....

A North Indian classic, Palak Paneer blends soft cottage cheese cubes in a smooth spinach gravy infused with garlic and mild spices. Rich, creamy and wholesome, it brings together health and indulgence in every bite...


Start your day with Palak Paneer!..


Ingredients....

Palak Paneer:

  • Palak (Spinach) Leaves – 1 bunch
  • Butter – 2 tbsp (1 for masala, 1 for curry)
  • Cumin Seeds – 1 tsp
  • Onion – 1, finely chopped
  • Green Chili – 2, finely chopped
  • Garlic – 4 cloves
  • Ginger – small piece, chopped
  • Tomato – 2, chopped
  • Turmeric Powder – 1/4 tsp
  • Kashmiri Chili Powder – 1 tsp
  • Coriander Powder – 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala – 1 tsp
  • Water – 1 cup (adjust as needed)
  • Salt – 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Paneer – 200 gm
  • Fresh Cream – for garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Boil a pot of water and blanch palak leaves for 2-3 minutes. Strain and keep aside.
  2. Step 2: Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds, then onions, green chilies, ginger, garlic; sauté until golden.
  3. Step 3: Add chopped tomatoes and cook well. Let the mixture cool.
  4. Step 4: Grind the cooled masala and blanched palak leaves together to make a fine paste.
  5. Step 5: Heat another tbsp butter in the pan, add turmeric, chilli, coriander, cumin powders, and garam masala; sauté briefly.
  6. Step 6: Add ground palak-masala paste, pour in water, and add salt. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Add paneer pieces, close and cook for another 5 minutes on low flame.
  8. Step 8: Garnish with fresh cream and serve hot with roti or rice.
