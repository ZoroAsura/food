Paneer Tikka....

Marinated in spiced yogurt and grilled to perfection, paneer tikka is smoky, tender and bursting with flavor. A favorite from the tandoor, it captures the true spirit of Indian barbecue...


Start your day with Paneer Tikka!..


Ingredients....

Paneer Tikka Ingredients:

  • Mustard Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Besan (Gram Flour) – 2 tbsp
  • Kashmiri Red Chili Powder – ½ tbsp
  • Jeera Powder – ½ tsp
  • Garam Masala – ½ tsp
  • Kali Mirch (Black Pepper) – ½ tsp
  • Chat Masala – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Kasoori Methi – ½ tsp
  • Ginger Garlic Paste – 1 tsp
  • Dahi (Curd) – 4 tbsp
  • Lemon – 1 (juiced)
  • Green & Yellow Capsicum – 7-8 pieces
  • Onion – 1 (shelled)
  • Paneer – 200g
  • Water – to adjust consistency
  • Charcoal – 1 piece
  • Oil – ½ tsp

    • Gravy Ingredients:

  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Onion – 1 cup (chopped)
  • Salt – to taste
  • Ginger Garlic Paste – 1 tsp
  • Tomato – 1 cup (chopped)
  • Oil – 2 tbsp (extra)
  • Black Cardamom – 3
  • Black Pepper – ½ tsp
  • Coriander Powder – 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder – ½ tsp
  • Green Chilli – 3 (slit)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves – 2-3 tbsp
  • Leftover Tikka Marinade
  • Corn Starch – 1 tsp (dissolved in 2 tbsp water)
  • Sugar – ½ tsp
  • Cream – 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Prepare the marinade with mustard oil, besan, Kashmiri chili, dry spices, ginger garlic paste, curd, lemon juice, and mix diced capsicum, onion, and paneer. Smoke with charcoal if desired, and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Grill or pan-roast the marinated paneer and veggies until lightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: For gravy, heat oil and add chopped onions and salt. Fry till golden brown. Add ginger garlic paste, sauté, then add chopped tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Step 4: Make a paste of the cooked masala with some water.
  5. Step 5: In a pan, heat extra oil, add black cardamom, then the made onion-tomato paste. Cook till thick and oil releases.
  6. Step 6: Add black pepper, coriander powder, red chilli powder, green chillies, and fresh coriander. Mix and sauté.
  7. Step 7: Add leftover tikka marinade, corn starch slurry, sugar, and cream. Mix and cook till thick.
  8. Step 8: Add grilled paneer tikka and veggies and a splash of water. Simmer, taste and finish with coriander and serve hot!
